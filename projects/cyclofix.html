Cyclofix

Website Repository

Open Summer of Code is a 4-week summer programme focused on building open source projects of public utility.

Commissioned by Brussels Mobility, my team and I built Cyclofix: a web app to find and update information on cycling infrastructure on OpenStreetMap.

Because of its success Pieter Vander Vennet – one of the team members – continued working on a generalized version of the app known as MapComplete, which is now used to map everything from AED locations to rainbow pedestrian crossings.
